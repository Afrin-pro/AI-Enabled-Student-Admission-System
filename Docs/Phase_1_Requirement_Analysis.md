# Phase 1 – Requirement Analysis & Planning

## Objective

The objective of this phase is to understand the business problem, identify stakeholder requirements, define the project scope, and prepare a high-level solution architecture for the **Student Admissions & Support Portal**.

## Business Background

Educational institutes often rely on spreadsheets or disconnected software to manage students, courses, admissions, and approvals. These fragmented systems lead to duplicate records, poor visibility, delayed approvals, and increased administrative effort.

Salesforce was selected as the CRM platform for this project because it offers:

- Secure cloud infrastructure
- Declarative automation
- Role-based security
- Reporting and dashboards
- AI capabilities through Agentforce

## Project Vision

Develop a centralized Salesforce application that manages the complete student admission lifecycle, reducing manual work through Salesforce automation and AI.

## Key Challenges

- Multiple disconnected systems
- Manual admission tracking
- Duplicate student records
- Slow approval processes
- Limited reporting capability
- Lack of AI assistance

## Proposed Solution

The Student Admissions & Support Portal will provide:

- Student Management
- Course Management
- Enrollment Tracking
- Approval Automation
- Role-Based Security
- Reports & Dashboards
- Agentforce AI Assistance

## Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Administrator | Configures Salesforce and manages users |
| Admission Counselor | Registers students and manages enrollments |
| Approver | Reviews and approves admissions |
| Student | Submits enrollment requests |
| Management | Monitors reports and dashboards |

## Deliverables

- Requirement Analysis
- Business Requirement Document
- Project Scope
- High-Level Architecture
- Functional Modules
- Initial Planning Documentation

## Success Criteria

- Centralized student data
- Secure access control
- Automated admissions
- Real-time reporting
- AI-powered assistance
